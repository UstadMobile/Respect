# Browse lessons via app link

## Summary
- Admin logs in to the App
- Adds app using external manifest link
- Verifies app is added successfully
- Opens app and browses lessons
- Opens and validates a lesson content

---

## Flow A: Add app from link
- Admin logs in
- Clicks **Add from Link**
- Enters app manifest URL
- Proceeds to next step

---

## Flow B: Verify app details
- Validate **App detail** screen
- Confirm app name and option to add app
- Click **Add App**

---

## Flow C: Verify app in Apps list
- Navigate to **Apps** section
- Confirm newly added app is visible
- Open the app

---

## Flow D: Browse lessons
- Open **Lessons** tab
- Select **Grade 1**
- Choose lesson from list
- Verify lesson details

---

## Flow E: Open lesson content
- Open selected lesson
- Verify lesson title and metadata
- Launch lesson content
- Confirm content loads successfully
- Close lesson
